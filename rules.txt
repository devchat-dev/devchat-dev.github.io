As a DevChat member I agree to:
1. Be friendly, respectful and welcoming to all members.  
2. Use a respectful username and profile picture.  
3. Keep discussions relevant to the channel’s topic. Usually programming related.  
4. Be ready to help. Be friendly when helping.  
5. Not discriminate against gender, color, nationality, or anything else. We’re all human.  
6. Not insult or provoke any member.  
7. Keep tempers under control.  
8. Not harass anyone or make them feel uncomfortable.  
9. Not use sexual or violent language. Sexual content is not allowed.  
10. Not advertise any commercial product.
